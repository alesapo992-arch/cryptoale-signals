What Are Tokenized Stocks?

Tokenized stocks are digital representations of traditional stock shares, often in the form of perpetual futures contracts. On Bitget, these are USDT-margined perpetual futures tied to the performance of underlying stocks or indices, without an expiry date. [](grok_render_citation_card_json={"cardIds":["91c828"]}) Traders can go long or short, benefiting from price movements while using leverage to amplify positions. This setup combines the accessibility of crypto trading with exposure to global equities, such as tech giants and ETFs.

Unlike spot trading, perpetual futures use funding rates to align with spot prices, enabling 24/5 trading (aligned with U.S. market hours). [](grok_render_citation_card_json={"cardIds":["1038f3"]}) Bitget's offering includes aggregated price feeds for transparency and reduced manipulation risks.
