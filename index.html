📜 Order Types Explained

  • Limit Order: Executes only at your chosen price or better.
  • Market Order: Executes immediately at current price; may cause slippage.
  • Trigger Order: Activates when a set price is reached.
  • Trailing Stop: Moves with price to secure profits.
